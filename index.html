About Liftiq

Liftiq is a minimalist workout tracker designed to help you implement progressive overload in your strength training. Our app focuses on simplicity and effectiveness, helping you track your progress without unnecessary complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my workouts?

Simply open the app, select an exercise, and enter your sets, reps, and weights. The app will automatically calculate your total volume and show your previous performance to guide your progression.

How does iCloud sync work?

Liftiq automatically syncs your data across all your devices using iCloud. Make sure you're signed into iCloud on your devices and have granted the necessary permissions.

Can I import my existing workout data?

Yes! You can import your workout data from CSV files. Go to the Exercises tab, tap the + button, and select "Import Data" to get started.
